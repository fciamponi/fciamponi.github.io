About

Biologist turned Data Scientist, with 8+ years of experience in bioinformatics. My expertise lies in integrative analysis of high- throughput biological data ("multi-omics"), but I also have extensive experience in dealing with high-performance computing clusters, statistics, high dimensional datasets, machine learning, network models and other related methods. I have a MSc in Genetics and a PhD in Bioinformatics by the University of Campinas.
